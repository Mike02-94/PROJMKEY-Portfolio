About Me

I’m a hands-on creative who thrives at the intersection of tech and imagination. Over the years, I’ve worn many hats—supporting customers through complex tech issues, investigating fraud with a sharp eye, and building sleek, responsive websites using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Beyond the screen, I dive into audio mastering for indie musicians and craft digital illustrations that turn ideas into visuals with personality. Whether it’s solving a problem or starting something from scratch, I bring curiosity, adaptability, and a genuine love for learning to everything I do.

Looking ahead, I’m chasing a dream that’s been with me for years: joining a game development team that’s breaking new ground in storytelling and design. Games have always fascinated me—not just for the fun, but for the way they connect people and spark imagination. I want to help create those moments. Every project, every late-night code session, and every sketch gets me closer to that goal. I’m building the skills and mindset now so that when the right opportunity arrives, I’ll be ready to hit start.
